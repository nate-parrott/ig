Quick and simple.

Print out our special camera-ready answer sheets, enter the correct answer choices into our app, and you're ready to start scanning quizzes with your iPhone. We'll deal with scoring and keeping track of results.

Learn more in the frequently asked questions.

Detailed score reports.

InstaGrade automatically calculates a class average for each question, builds a score distribution bar graph, and generates a summary sheet for each student, with their grade and the questions they missed.

Free to get started.

InstaGrade is free to download and scan quizzes up to 24 questions long. For longer tests, a one-time purchase of $1.99 US (or equivalent in your currency) unlocks a 56-question answer sheet.
