Frequently Asked Questions

My scans are innacurate, or images of them are blurry. What can I do?
A couple things:
  • Scan in bright, even light, without shadows. If you see a lightning bolt in the top-left corner of your screen during scanning, use that to turn on the flashlight.
  • Make sure quizzes aren't creased, folded or curved.
  • Make sure there aren't any stray marks on the page.
  • Hold the scanner in front of the page for a little longer. InstaGrade tries to capture the least-blurry image it can, so the longer you let it scan a page, the more likely you are to get a non-blurry image.
What can I do with the results of a quiz?
Right now, you can view individual quiz results in the app, or visit our website for more in-depth analysis, including downloading an Excel file containing results. If you have a specific format you'd like to export to, or a web service you'd like us to upload to automatically, please leave feedback.
Can I create quizzes that are multiple pages?
Sure. We'll just ask you to scan pages in order.
